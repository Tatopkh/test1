About us

The UEFA Champions League (previously known as the European Cup and mostly abbreviated
worldwide as the UCL) is an annual club association football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations
(UEFA) and contested by top-division European clubs, deciding the competition winners through a round robin group stage to qualify for a double-legged knockout format, and a single leg final.
